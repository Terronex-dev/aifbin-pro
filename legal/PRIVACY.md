# Privacy Policy

**AIF-BIN Pro**  
**Last Updated:** February 1, 2026

---

Terronex.dev ("we", "us", or "our") built AIF-BIN Pro with privacy as a core principle.

## What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or store:

- The content of your files
- Your AIF-BIN files or their contents
- Any extracted text, tables, images, or metadata
- Your AI API keys (stored locally only)
- Personal identifiers (unless you contact support)

## What We Do Collect

**Nothing by default.** AIF-BIN Pro is local-first software.

If you choose to:
- **Contact support:** We retain your email for support purposes
- **Report bugs:** We may receive error logs you voluntarily submit

## How Files Are Processed

- **All processing happens locally** on your device
- **Cloud AI mode (optional):** When you enable cloud AI, content is sent to third-party providers (Google, OpenAI, etc.) subject to their privacy policies
- **Local AI mode:** Everything stays on your computer
- **We never see your files**

## Third-Party Services

When you use cloud AI modes, you are subject to third-party privacy policies:
- [Google Gemini Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)

## Your Rights

- **Delete:** All data is local — delete files anytime
- **Opt out:** Use local mode only
- **Export:** Use the export command to get your data
